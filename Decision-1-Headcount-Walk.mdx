<Tooltip tip="Meridian Staffing is a fictional 13-person RPO specializing in U.S. federal government hiring, used throughout this guide to demonstrate RequisitionIQ." headline="Who is Meridian Staffing?">Meridian Staffing</Tooltip> has 29 open reqs for Forest Services and needs to know **where their client's headcount will land on February 1st**, since the client has a major project initiative they need staffed.

RequisitionIQ uses a <Tooltip tip="A step-by-step calculation that projects ending headcount by starting with current headcount and applying known workforce changes, such as transfers, terminations, accepted offers, and predicted requisition fill dates.">headcount waterfall</Tooltip> and a <Tooltip tip="A statistical model that estimates the probability an open requisition will fill by a future date based on historical hiring patterns and the requisition's characteristics.">survival model</Tooltip> to predict the likelihood  open reqs will fill by the target end date.

**The output is the predicted fill waterfall model below.**

<Card title="Definitions" icon="circle-info">
  Outlined below are Steps 1 to 7 explaining the walk from Current to Ending Headcount. 

  <Callout icon="square-1">
    **Current HC:** Total number of active employees in the organization you want to model.
  </Callout>

  <Callout icon="square-2">
    **Transfers IN:** Active employees moving into the organization you selected to model.
  </Callout>

  <Callout icon="square-3">
    **Transfers OUT:** Active employees moving out of the organization you selected to model.
  </Callout>

  <Callout icon="square-4">
    **Terms:** Future terminations the will be successfully completed by the <Tooltip tip="The configured Ending HC Date for the headcount walk and survival model." headline="Ending HC Date">target date</Tooltip>.
  </Callout>

  <Callout icon="square-5">
    <Tooltip tip='All offers that have a Start Date between the Current and Ending HC Date. Offers that are "past due" are excluded and require their start date updated to be included in the model.'>Offers Accepted</Tooltip>**:** All offers that have a Start Date between the Current and <Tooltip tip="The configured Ending HC Date for the headcount walk and survival model." headline="Ending HC Date">target date</Tooltip>.
  </Callout>

  <Callout icon="square-6">
    **Predicted Fills:** The <Tooltip tip="A statistical model that estimates the probability an open requisition will fill by a future date based on historical hiring patterns and the requisition's characteristics.">Survival Model</Tooltip> is used to predict when open requisition will fill.
  </Callout>

  <Callout icon="square-7">
    **Ending HC:** The forecasted ending headcount with a confidence level of 95%.
  </Callout>
</Card>
