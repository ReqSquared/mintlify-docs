<Tooltip tip="Meridian Staffing is a fictional 13-person RPO specializing in U.S. federal government hiring, used throughout this guide to demonstrate RequisitionIQ." headline="Who is Meridian Staffing?">Meridian Staffing</Tooltip> has 29 open reqs for Forest Services and needs to know w**here their client's headcount will land on February 1st**, since the client has a major project initiative they need staffed.

RequisitionIQ uses a <Tooltip tip="A step-by-step calculation that projects ending headcount by starting with current headcount and applying known workforce changes, such as transfers, terminations, accepted offers, and predicted requisition fill dates.">headcount waterfall</Tooltip> and a <Tooltip tip="A statistical model that estimates the probability an open requisition will fill by a future date based on historical hiring patterns and the requisition's characteristics.">survival model</Tooltip> to estimate which open reqs are likely to fill by the target date.

The output is a forecasted ending headcount, plus the open requisitions most likely to contribute to that number.

<Callout icon="cube">
  **The Headcount Walk Equation:**

  `Current HC + IN - OUT - Terms + Offers Accepted + Predicted Fills = Ending HC`
</Callout>
